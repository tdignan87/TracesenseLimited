About Us

Tracesense Limited is a privately owned Scottish company launched in 2019. We are a niche software development company, not a reseller of other companies software and we do all our software in Scotland. Tracesense is a proud family business, run by the founder Tom Dignan alongside a dedicated team.

Tom has over 10 years in the manufacturing industries from operative to Management levels so we understand what is important to manufacturers.

Delivering a personal professional service is what we believe in and we focus on how important the system is in your business. We will work with you to achieve the perfect solution for your needs.

We strive for close working relationships with customers and our aim is helping them achieve the best out of systems and not promise them things we don't deliver.
If you want to deal with a company where the staff are professional who listen to your needs then please talk to us.
